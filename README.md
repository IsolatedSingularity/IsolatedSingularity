### Featured Industry Projects

#### [Léonne: Modular Consensus Networks](https://github.com/btq-ag/Leonne)
A modular framework for simulating consensus networks. It utilizes post-quantum topologically protected algorithms to generate resilient cryptographic proofs.
* **Focus:** Blockchain security, PQC, and scalable consensus protocols.

#### Topological Quantum Neural Networks (TQNN) (Repository coming soon)
Applying topological quantum field theory concepts to model and optimize information flow in quantum systems.
* **Focus:** Improving generalization in deep learning through global and topological learning instead of black box evolution.

#### Quantum Circuit Builder (Internal: Release Pending)
A tool designed for mapping non-planar molecules to quantum circuits and implementing variational quantum eigensolvers (VQE).
* **Focus:** Ground state energy calculation and automated circuit optimization.

### Current Research Focus
* **Many-Body Systems:** Systematic verification of the eigenstate thermalization hypothesis across integrable and non-integrable models.
* **Thermalization Behavior:** Investigating thermalization through exact diagonalization, level spacing statistics, and scaling analysis of thermalization times with Hilbert space dimension.
* **Error Correction:** Axiomatization of holographic quantum error correction codes with non-compact Hilbert spaces.
