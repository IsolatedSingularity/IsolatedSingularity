# Jeffrey Morais (IsolatedSingularity)

**Head of Quantum Software @ BTQ** **MSc Student in Theoretical Physics @ University of Victoria**

I bridge the gap between high energy physics and deployable quantum software. My work focuses on the intersection of Topological Data Analysis (TDA), Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC), and Quantum Algorithms.

---

### Featured Quantum Research and Industry Projects

#### [Léonne: Modular Consensus Networks](https://github.com/btq-ag/Leonne)
A modular framework for simulating consensus networks. It utilizes post-quantum topologically protected algorithms to generate resilient cryptographic proofs.
* **Focus:** Blockchain security, PQC, and scalable consensus protocols.

#### Topological Quantum Neural Networks (TQNN) (Repository coming soon)
Researching the use of topological quantum field theory concepts to model and optimize information flow in quantum systems.
* **Focus:** Improving generalization in deep learning through global and topological learning instead of black box evolution.

#### Quantum Circuit Builder (Internal: Release Pending)
A specialized tool for mapping non-planar molecules to quantum circuits and implementing variational quantum eigensolvers (VQE).
* **Focus:** Ground state energy calculation and automated circuit optimization.
